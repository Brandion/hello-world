Pokemon GO 0.193.3

Pokemon GO - game, which became very popular before the full release. The excitement is explained by the fact that the application is built on a fresh and original concept of augmented reality. The player will have to catch virtual pokemon in the real world, after installing the game the user will have to customize the appearance of the main character and choose a name. Then there will be a short tutorial, where the English language explains the key moments of the game. To consolidate the acquired knowledge, you have to catch your first pokemon, which will be close to you. After a short lesson begins the basic gameplay. The main screen displays the 3D map you wish to navigate to hunt. Works with Pokemon GO only with good mobile internet and GPS for better accuracy and better mapping. The player has to move around the city and look for places to hide, “pocket animals”.
